Aim of the website 


A current analysis of our existing online presence reveals a lack of cohesive information and outdated content. Our current website is not optimized for mobile devices, making it difficult for users to access information on-the-go. Furthermore, our social media presence is fragmented, with inconsistent posting schedules and lack of engagement. To address these gaps, our new website will be designed with a responsive layout, ensuring seamless user experience across devices. The website will feature: 

Project portfolio showcasing our infrastructure development initiatives 

News and updates section for latest developments and success stories 

Stakeholder engagement platform for feedback and collaboration 

Donation and volunteer sign-up functionality 

Resource library with relevant reports, documents, and publications 

By developing a robust online presence, we aim to increase our reach, engage with our stakeholders, and ultimately drive more support for our rural infrastructure development initiatives. As noted by the South African National Planning Commission, "investing in infrastructure is critical for economic growth and development" (NPC, 2012). Our website will play a vital role in achieving this goal.
